Thank you for reading Blackthorn Field Notes.

I welcome thoughtful reader notes, accessibility feedback, site or security concerns, publishing-related messages, and messages about the writing. I read what I can, but I cannot promise a personal reply to every message.

## General Reader Notes

Reader notes are welcome through the contact form.

This is a free contact option for brief, respectful messages about the writing, the site, accessibility issues, or general feedback. A reply is not guaranteed.

## Supporter / Community Access

If you want to support the work more directly, follow or support me on Ko-fi.

Supporter updates, behind-the-scenes notes, and future community options may live there.

[Support on Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/cjblackthorn){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}

## Professional Inquiries

Blackthorn Field Notes is the public writing and publishing home.

Professional work, business inquiries, collaboration, consulting, systems support, and future offerings belong under Blackthorn Enterprises.

[Visit Blackthorn Enterprises](https://blackthorn.enterprises/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}

## Boundaries

This contact page is not for emergency help, crisis support, harassment, spam, or demands for immediate personal response.

Please keep messages clear, respectful, and reasonably brief.
