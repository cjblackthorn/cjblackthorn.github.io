# Services

Blackthorn Field Notes is primarily a public writing and publishing space. I also occasionally consider limited relationship and life coaching, collaboration, writing, accessibility, technology, and systems-related work on a case-by-case basis.

Availability is limited. Any work that requires my time, attention, planning, review, consultation, or emotional labor depends on my capacity, schedule, and whether the request is a good fit.

## Relationship and Life Coaching

I occasionally offer relationship and life coaching support on a limited, case-by-case basis.

This work may include support with communication, boundaries, conflict patterns, relationship repair, self-advocacy, life transitions, systems navigation, practical emotional clarity, and understanding recurring relational dynamics.

Coaching availability is limited and requires flexible scheduling. Submitting an inquiry does not guarantee availability, acceptance, or a personal response.

## What Coaching Is Not

Coaching is not therapy, crisis support, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, diagnosis, emergency help, or a replacement for licensed professional care.

I do not provide emergency support through this site. I also do not offer unlimited access, immediate responses, or ongoing private support unless that has been intentionally discussed and agreed to.

## Professional Inquiries

Professional inquiries may include writing, editing, publishing, collaboration, accessibility feedback, technology/systems consultation, advocacy-oriented work, or media-related requests.

Professional work, consulting, review, collaboration, or scheduled time may require a paid agreement.

## How to Inquire

Use the contact form and choose the reason that best fits your message.

For coaching, choose **Relationship / life coaching inquiry**.

For professional work, choose **Professional inquiry** or **Publishing / media inquiry**.

Please keep your message clear, respectful, and reasonably brief.

## Boundaries

I care deeply about the work I do, but care does not mean unlimited access.

I reserve the right to decline requests that are outside my capacity, outside my scope, unclear, unsafe, inappropriate, or not a good fit.
