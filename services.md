## What Belongs Here

Blackthorn Field Notes remains focused on essays, resources, updates, accessibility notes, publishing information, and reader-facing site information.

Use this site when you want to read the work, subscribe, browse categories, find accessibility information, or send a reader note.

## What Belongs There

Blackthorn Enterprises is the better place for professional context, business inquiries, project work, accessibility or systems consulting, collaboration, and any future services or offerings connected to C. J. Blackthorn's work.

That separation keeps this site clearer for readers while still making the larger Blackthorn ecosystem easier to find.
